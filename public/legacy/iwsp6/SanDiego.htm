San Diego...

Enjoy!
 

So you are planning to be in San Diego several days before or after IWSP6, or you are thinking about bringing your family with you, or you are also attending the Society for Neuroscience annual meeting and you are wondering what there is to do in San Diego.  The list is long and includes SeaWorld, San Diego Zoo, museums, beaches, water sports and much more.  Here is a link to SanDiego.org which is "The Official Travel Resource for the San Diego Region."  It's all there - maps, activities and special offers. 

Another resource for making your plans is The San Diegan which is a website full of suggestions for activities, restaurants, attractions, museums, etc. and has coupons for discounts and special offers.  You can request to have a free copy of their magazine sent to you or you will find a copy in your room at the Bahia.

Watersports!  Since the Bahia is physically on Mission Bay and only two blocks from the Pacific Ocean, watersports are definitely to be considered.  This includes stand up paddle boards, body boards, peddle boats, kayaks, funcats, powerboats, catamarans and sailboats.  Resort Watersports has a kiosk at the Bahia to rent these items as well as bicycles, Segways, and more.
