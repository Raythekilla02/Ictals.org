register

 
To register for the Sanibel Symposium go to www.regonline.com/sanibelsymposium and complete the registration process.

Openings are limited so register early.

 

To make hotel reservations at the Casa Ybel Resort, call them directly at +1-800-276-4753  or  +1-239-472-3145.  Tell them you are attending the Alliance for Epilepsy Research event which is being held there February 11-12, 2010.  Rooms at the group rate of $289/night are limited but there are other nearby hotels on Sanibel Island and in Ft. Myers.
