Our Mission

International Conference for Technology and AnaLysis of Seizures (ICTALS) is a series of conferences dedicated to advancing innovative, multidisciplinary approaches for understanding, detecting, predicting, and treating seizures.

ICTALS brings together researchers, clinicians, engineers, and industry partners to share knowledge, present new ideas, and collaborate on technologies that support seizure analysis and care. The conference aims to bridge fundamental research and clinical practice, helping to improve diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment outcomes for people living with epilepsy and related conditions.

This series of conferences is facilitated by the Alliance for Epilepsy Research.
