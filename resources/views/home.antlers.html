Our Mission

International Conference for Technology and Analysis of Seizures (ICTALS) is a series of conferences focusing on innovative, multi‑discipline solutions for the understanding, detection, prediction, and treatment of seizures.

The conference brings together researchers, clinicians, engineers, and industry professionals to share knowledge, present new ideas, and collaborate on advancing technologies and approaches in seizure analysis and care. ICTALS aims to support progress in research and practical solutions that can improve diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment outcomes.
